Biography

A Legacy of
Operational Excellence

With three decades of extensive experience in the public sector, N.V. Raghubabu stands as a pillar of efficiency and integrity at Western Coalfields Limited (WCL), a subsidiary of Coal India Limited.

Currently serving as General Manager in the Material Management Department, he oversees the complex supply chains that fuel one of India's most critical energy sectors. His tenure is marked by a strategic approach to procurement, utilizing modern e-tendering and reverse auction mechanisms to ensure transparency. He specializes in optimizing procurement lifecycles, reducing turnaround times for Heavy Earth Moving Machinery (HEMM) spares, and maintaining strict adherence to PSU vigilance norms.
